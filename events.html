All Day Events
Department Event
Auto Colision Repair Technology Experience a career that really shines with the Auto Body Program. Hammer out a dent, spray some color with an airbrush, or paint a car using the Virtual Paint Simulator!
Agriculture There's something for everyone at the Agriculture Program booth! Watch baby chicks hatch and/or see how fast you can field strip a chain saw and put it back together! Fastest time wins a prize! There will also be ag equipment demos.
Automotive Experience a career in the fast lane with the Automotive Program. See a transmission from the inside-out, test out your diagnostic skills on an electrical system trainer, or learn to torque a V-8 engine like the pros!
Aviation Come visit with us about careers in aviation. Compete with other aspiring aviators in the paper and balsa wood airplane contests for a prize, and give the flight simulator a try!
Computer Information Sciences Discover careers in software development! Play a 2D side scroller, learn about the Internet of Things, prgram a Rasberry Pi, explore Python code, and find out what Rubber Duck Debugging is all about!
Construction Technology We'll give you actual blueprints so you can assemble a 1 inch equals 1 foot scaled down model of a house. Visit with us about careers in construction.
Drafting and Design Technology Experience a career in Drafting and Design Technology by being inside a computer-generated simulation of a student designed luxury home with our virtual reality headset.  See and experience 3D printing with our Mini Replicator Makerbot printers.  You can also participate in hands on activities to test your problem solving and critical thinking skills.  Oh, and don't forget to pick up your free OTC key chain made with our 3D printers.
Diesel Technology Experience a career for the long haul with the Diesel Program. See what’s going on underneath a big-rig, tune a diesel engine that’s out of the truck, or get hands-on with the Auxiliary Power Unit!
Early Childhood Development Explore careers in earlychildhood development through play! Paint canvas, dig into sensory tubs, shoot baskets, dance, sing and more!
Electrical Come visit with us about the career possibilities in Electrical Trades. While you're at our booth, try wiring a circuit using a diagram and see if it works!
Electrical Dustribution Systems Stop by and see what it takes to climb an electric pole and learn more about the EDS degree!
Electronic Media Production Discover the magic of movie making as you record your own sound effects for a scene in a short film.
Fire Science Technology Make your way outside and check out one of Springfield Fire Department's fire engines! Stay on for a little while and compete with other students to see who can dress out in fire fighting turn out gear the fastest and/or participate in a fire hose roll competition.
Graphic Design Technology Try out the tools of the Graphic Design trade: 1) a “special effects” photo (starring you) that includes a print you can take home, and 2) a backpack with special art you apply to the material using a heat press…which is also yours to keep!
Health Sciences Interested in a career in healthcare? If so, plan to visit us. While you're here, participate in a fun CPR challenge, check out our high-tech training manikins and visit with instructors about career possibilities.
Heating, Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Come visit with us about this high-demand technical field and try your hand at measuring temperature and pressure in a heat pump.
Industrial Systems Technology Learn about this high-demand technical field and about what it takes to keep important industrial machinery operating. We'll have fun activities and demos. Come check it out!
Machine Tool Technology Learn from student machinists about how computers are used to control machine tools such as lathes and mills. There will also be giveaways and a 5-axis computer numerical control machining demonstration.
Networking Technology The Networking Technology program will showcase a number of activities/demos.  Various aspects and examples of the Internet of Things (IoT) – including 3D Printing, as well as aspects of computer and network hardware.
Welding Technology Visit with us at the Welding booth and see if this is the career for you! Try out the ever-popular welding simulator or try your hand at some actual welding.
